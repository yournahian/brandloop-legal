BrandLoop User Data Deletion Instructions

We believe in providing you with full control over your data. You can request the deletion of your BrandLoop account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your BrandLoop account and all of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your BrandLoop dashboard.
  2. Navigate to your "Account Settings" or "Profile" page.
  3. Find and click on the "Delete My Account" button.
  4. You will be asked to confirm your decision. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete your profile, earned points, badges, and all tracked content history from our platform.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon confirming your request, we will initiate a process to permanently delete the following information from our active systems:

  • Your public profile information (Name, Facebook Profile Picture).
  • The email address associated with your account.
  • All content we have tracked that you posted tagging our brand partners.
  • Your Mindshare score history.
  • All earned points, badges, and rewards history.
